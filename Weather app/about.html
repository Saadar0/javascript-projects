About us

Welcome to our weather app, your go-to weather companion! As a student passionate about meteorology and technology, I created this app to provide accurate and detailed weather insights. Our Home Page offers real-time temperature updates, an hourly forecast, and essential details like humidity, wind speed, and atmospheric pressure. For astronomy enthusiasts, our Astronomy Page features moon phase information,solar and lunar times, and other celestial details to keep you connected with the sky. Whether you're planning your day or stargazing at night, Our app has you covered with reliable and easy-to-use weather data.
